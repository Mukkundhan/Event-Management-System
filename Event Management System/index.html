UI/UX Design

Rules

Individual Event

Eligibility: II, III Years (CSE & CSD).

ROUND 1: UI/UX Design Presentation

  • This round will consist of the presentation.
  • The paper should be preferably in a power-point presentation(no word documents allowed)
  • The presentation should not exceed 10 slides
  • The max time limit for presentation is 10 mins with a further extension of about 5 mins for discussion
  • The paper presentation abstracts should be sent to 210701170@rajalakshmi.edu.in.
  • The decision taken by event co-ordinators is final and binding
  • The paper must be in .pdf or .doc or .docx formats.
  • The paper must be sent through mail. The instructions are as follows:
    1. The subject must be the title of the paper.
    2. The body of the mail should include - Name and Institute of participant (starting from the first author and going till the third one), Department and Year, Contact numbers, Email addresses.

Last date to send in the paper: 21st December 2023

Game Jam

Team Event

Eligibility: II, III Years (CSE & CSD).

  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 participants and a maximum of 3 participants.

Code Relay

Test your mind and push your limits to solve various problems and come up with the best possible code you can and implement it at the fastest pace you can type.

Rules

Team Event

Eligibility: II, III Years (CSE & CSD).

Language: C, C++, Java, Python

ROUND 1: Entry level round

  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 participants and a maximum of 3 participants.
  • All team members must be from the same year.
  • 3 Programming Questions.
  • Selection will be based on the maximum number of questions solved in the least amount of time.
  • The average score of all team members will be calculated for shortlisting.

ROUND 2: Final Round

  • 3 Programming Questions.
  • The relay order of the teammates will be collected prior and it cannot be changed once the round begins.
  • Each question needs to be answered individually. 30 minutes will be provided for solving each question.
  • Each Team member is provided with only one question one after the another and cannot help each other while solving the questions.
  • If a teammate is unable to complete the given question within 30 minutes then the corresponding status of that question will be considered for evaluation and the next team member can proceed further.
Code Relay
